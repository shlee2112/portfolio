Savia Design System


A Novel Design Language for Crafting Clinically Focused Experiences

Overview

"In the demanding hospital setting, clinicians frequently navigate vital patient and clinical data. However, our inflexible design assets failed to align with clinical protocols and lacked a clear visual hierarchy, impeding the user's ability to access critical information efficiently. As a result, I led the design team in developing a revolutionary design language tailored to empower clinicians, enabling seamless focus on crucial tasks and driving goal attainment. This involved defining foundational principles, establishing design tokens, and curating a library of reusable assets, fostering a unified and adaptable design approach.

Year: 2023 ~ 2024

My Role: Lead Designer

Design Principles

By leveraging insights gained from our research and iterative design processes, we successfully formulated our unique set of design principles, serving as guiding beacons for our design language.

Foundations

To ensure interface elements are logically organized, directing users' attention to critical details first, I established four emphasis level rules.

Through the strategic assignment of colors to each level, we achieved consistent and predictable interfaces.

Building upon the concept, I developed comprehensive guidelines for foundational elements including color, typography, spacing, motion, and more.

Design Tokens

I utilized foundational elements to establish design tokens, serving as the fundamental building blocks for all UI elements. Through the creation of global, semantic, and component tokens, I meticulously managed the consistency and scalability of the UI.

Components

Utilizing our unique design principles and foundational elements, I iteratively developed components designed to empower our users.

Handoff Process

Prior to finalizing the asset handoff, I meticulously documented the construction of each component using tokens and maintained a detailed changelog.

UX Audits

Beforereleasing a new version, we conducted a thorough UX audit to ensure that all components were built correctly in a test environment.
