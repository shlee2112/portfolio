E-Path Plus

Improving cancer registry experiences

Overview

In order to improve usability when analyzing and abstracting cancer patient data, we redesigned the original E-Path Plus, which utilizes NLP capabilities to streamline the cancer registry process.

Background

In the cancer registry industry, registrars play a vital role in the healthcare system by contributing to cancer research. They are responsible for collecting, abstracting and maintaining accurate records of cancer patient data.

E-Path Plus is a cancer registry application that utilizes AI technology to extract discrete information about the disease including cancer site, histologic type, staging information etc.

Problem Scope

Our design team conducted multiple rounds of interviews with two current users and two original engineers who developed this product. Additionally, we conducted internal design audits to identify opportunities for improving cancer registry experiences. And, we synthesized our findings into three major problems.

quiz

Excessive steps required for information access or action

Users had to drag panels to expand and collapse to reveal patient data. They also had to click multiple times to navigate and take action.

ads_click

Lack of visual cues

It was difficult for users to differentiate between discrete data and manually entered text through current visual cues, as well as to identify fields with new information or conflicts from different sources.

account_tree

Lack of information hierarchy

Registrars often struggled to find significant pieces of information when reviewing multiple medical documents due to overwhelming information lacking an order of importance.

The solution

Based on our analysis and strategy, we decided to completely overhaul the original application, which lacked scalability and relied on outdated visuals.

layers

Layout and content structure rework

We redesigned the layout to reduce unnecessary interactions for accessing and navigating the application. Also, we updated the content structure to enhance readability and streamline access to necessary information.

ads_click

Clear and consistent visual cues

Using the right color codes and visual cues for different data formats, we aid users in distinguishing between extracted and manually inputted information. Moreover, we incorporated clear annotations to provide instant insights.

ads_click

Clear visual hierarchy

By differentiating content and actionable items from the background, we establish a clear user path amidst overwhelming information and text, enabling users to focus and navigate effectively.
