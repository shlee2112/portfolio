Hi, I'm Sanghyun!

As an AI-driven UX designer, I deeply believe that embracing and combining information processing power and human-centered design can positively shape our daily lives. From Machine Perception to Natural Language Processing (NLP), I have designed multiple AI-powered experiences and products to solve user needs in a unique way. Also, I often code and develop a solution myself to deeply understand the superpowers and limitations of AI.


Outside of the design world, I am a rock climber who loves to explore and craves the rush of adrenaline at the top. Rock climbing is like solving a puzzle through body movements and different techniques. I especially love to hang out and collaborate with my friends to figure out how to reach the top.
