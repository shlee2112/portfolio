Hi, I'm Sanghyun!

I am a current master's student pursuing a degree in Human-Computer Interaction at the University of Michigan. My North Star is to leverage the information processing power of artificial intelligence and create new programs that can connect people at deeper interpersonal levels. Especially, driven by my industrial design background, I believe optimizing the use of the connected products can make a great impact on our human relationship.


For the Summer 2019, I had a unique experience with the Google Lens team as a UX Design intern. In the past, I have interned as a UX researcher at IBM Design, and worked as a UX designer at TmaxOS. Currently, as a Graduate Student Instructor (TA), I am instructing 42 graduate students to help understand how to develop websites and design for accessibility with POUR principles.


Outside of the design world, I am a rock climber who loves to explore and craves the rush of adrenaline at the top. Rock climbing is like solving a puzzle through body movements and different techniques. I especially love to hang out and collaborate with my friends to figure out how to reach the top.
